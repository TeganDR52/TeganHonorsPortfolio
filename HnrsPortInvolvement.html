Cool Things I'm Involved With!

Below is a list of all of the clubs and organizations I'm either a member of or volunteer for! They're all amazing groups of people, so I highly reccomend that you check them out if you have any interest in them!

I am currently a member of the UC CubeCats! They are a student-run organization that works on cube sattelites and weather balloons. I'm currently a member of their CATiSE program, and I'm hoping to advance to their CubeSat program this spring!

For more information, I encourage you to check out their very own website linked here! They're a very cool group, and have connections with NASA and other professionals in engineering!


I am also currently a member of the UC Self-Defense Club! They host self-defense classes on campus led by trained professionals. I joined because I'm short and look like an easy target, so it would be useful for me to know how to defend myself if I run into anyone dangerous out on the streets of Cincinnati.

For more information, I encourage you to check out their page on GetInvolvedUC linked here! They're open to everyone and have 3 classes listed on their page you can attend!


I am currently volunteering every Friday at the Cincinnati Hillel to help clean up after their Shabbat Evening Experiences! They are a center for young Jewish life in Cincinnati and are very friendly. They also make very good food!

For more information, I encourage you to check out their website linked here! They're very welcoming of newcomers and, if there's spots available, likely wouldn't mind a helping hand!
