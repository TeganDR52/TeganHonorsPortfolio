Daily Bug Art

If you couldn't already tell, I am a massive fan of bugs. Big or small, delicate or dangerous - I think they're unique and beautiful, and every time I'm outside in nature, they're the first thing to catch my eye. I also happen to possess some decently-trained art skill. So, I put two and two together, and started a series of bug artwork online for everyone to enjoy! Some are bugs local to the area, while others are species I found while fluttering through the web. One day I hope that more people find this artwork, and that they begin to see the beauty I see in them, and appreciate them more as a vital part of our world's ecosystems.

I have 50 days of daily bug art planned, so be sure to check back if you like what you see!
